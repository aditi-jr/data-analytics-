This project analyzes **Diwali sales data** to uncover customer purchasing behavior and business insights. Using **Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn**, the dataset is cleaned, explored, and visualized to identify trends in sales across different demographics and product categories.  

---

## 📂 Dataset  
- File: `Diwali Sales Data.csv`  
- Contains customer demographic details, orders, products, and amount spent.  
- Used for **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and visualization.  

---

## 📊 Features of Analysis  
✔ Data Cleaning & Preprocessing  
✔ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) with Pandas & Seaborn  
✔ Visualizations for:  
- **Sales vs Age Group**  
- **Top 10 States by Orders**  
- **Marital Status vs Purchase Behavior**  
- **Occupation-wise Purchases**  
- **Top Product Categories & Products Sold**  

---

## 📈 Key Insights  
- 👩‍🦰 **Married women (26–35 years)** are the highest buyers.  
- 🏙 Top states: **Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka**.  
- 💼 Occupations with higher spending: **IT, Healthcare, Aviation**.  
- 🛒 Most popular categories: **Food, Clothing, Electronics**.  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack  
- **Python** 🐍  
- **Pandas** → Data cleaning & manipulation  
- **Matplotlib** → Data visualization  
- **Seaborn** → Statistical plots
